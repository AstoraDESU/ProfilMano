Biografi

Mano Sakuragi (櫻木真乃 Sakuragi Mano) is one of the idols available in THE iDOLM@STER Shiny Colors. She is considered the protagonist and face of Shiny Colors as well as the main red representative of the branch. As the leader of illumination STARS, she makes up the main trio of the game along with Hiori Kazano and Meguru Hachimiya. She is voiced by Hitomi Sekine (関根瞳 Sekine Hitomi).
